Our Programs

Big Chance Camp & Showcase

Providing free and subsidized vouchers for athletes to attend premier sports camps, combines, and showcases.

Fuel Program

Addressing food insecurity among student-athletes by ensuring access to nutritious meals throughout the week.

Community Workshops

Free workshops on mental conditioning, college recruitment, and developing robust public sports programming.

Why This Matters

Communities of color often face barriers that hinder athletic potential. We believe in strong youth programs with quality coaching and seamless navigation of scholastic sports and college recruitment processes.

Our philosophy, inspired by ubuntu ("I am because we are"), emphasizes community connection and follows the Long-Term Athlete Development Program approach.
